Status: Beats Us 🤷

Leadership decisions are made loudly. Very loudly. From there, the timeline clocks out. Scenes overlap. Cause and effect stop returning emails. Portals open at the worst possible moments, and family therapy is replaced with cosmic escalation. Oh — remember Amit Jr. came from Q6? This arc is simply the next attempt to stop Q6 before it monetizes reality itself. Are these events sequential? Parallel? A quality control error? Officially: cosmic instability.
